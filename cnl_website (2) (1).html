The Challenge

Full-Length Measurement

Required capability to measure tube wall thickness along the entire length, not just select points or segments.

Multi-Angle Detection

Ability to assess tube deformation and thickness variation at multiple angles around the circumference.

Non-Destructive Testing

Measurements must be taken without damaging or altering the integrity of the pressure tubes.

Deformation Detection

System must reliably detect and quantify tube deformation patterns for safety assessment.

Previous System Limitations

⚠ No Zero Matching

Inability to establish reference points between measurements

⚠ Probe Tilt

Sensor misalignment causing inaccurate readings

⚠ Sensor Blocked

Obstructions preventing complete measurement coverage

Our Solution: Internal Rotating Probe System

Comprehensive Design

A state-of-the-art rotating probe mechanism that travels the full length of pressure tubes while simultaneously rotating 360° to capture multi-angle measurements.

Key Components

  • 360° Probe Rotation: Full circumferential scanning capability
  • Dual Lead Screws: Synchronized axial movement with gear synchronization
  • Time-of-Flight Sensors: Two independent distance measurement sensors
  • Rotary Encoders: Precise angle and position tracking
  • Lazy Susan Bearing: Tube rotation mechanism with precision clamps
